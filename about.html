About Us

Discover our mission, vision, and the passionate team driving change in Kisumu's water and sanitation landscape.

Our Mission

To empower youth to take action on water, sanitation, and hygiene issues in their communities.

Our Vision

A world where every community has access to clean water and sanitation, led by empowered youth leaders.

Our Core Values

Our core values guide our actions and define our approach to achieving our mission.

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of integrity in all our actions.

Collaboration

We work together, across boundaries, to meet the needs of our communities.

Innovation

We strive for innovation to improve the lives of those we serve.

What We Do

Learn about the various programs and initiatives we undertake to achieve our mission.

Water Access Programs

We provide clean and safe water access to communities in need.

Sanitation Solutions

Our sanitation solutions ensure healthy living conditions for all.

Youth Empowerment

Empowering youth to lead and create sustainable change in their communities.

Community Engagement

Engaging with communities to foster collaboration and mutual growth.
