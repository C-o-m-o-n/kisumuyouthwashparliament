About Us

Discover our mission, vision, and the passionate team driving change in Kisumu's water and sanitation landscape.

Our Mission

To advocate for and implement youth-led sustainable WASH initiatives

Our Vision

To be the leader in empowering youth to influence sustainable management and access of water, sanitation, and hygiene in Kisumu County.

Our Core Values

Our core values guide our actions and define our approach to achieving our mission.

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of integrity in all our actions.

Collaboration

We work together, across boundaries, to meet the needs of our communities.

Innovation

We strive for innovation to improve the lives of those we serve.
