Thematic Areas

Discover our key focus areas that drive our mission for sustainable water, sanitation, and hygiene solutions.

Our Key Thematic Areas

We focus on three interconnected thematic areas to create sustainable impact in water, sanitation, and hygiene.

Integrated Water Resource Management

We promote effective policies and management systems for sustainable water resources and services through:

  • Water Governance

    Advocating for inclusive water governance structures that involve youth in decision-making processes.

  • Water Conservation

    Promoting sustainable water use practices and conservation techniques in communities.

  • Water Access

    Implementing projects to improve access to clean water in underserved areas of Kisumu County.

5,000+ beneficiaries

Waste Management

We ensure accessible and proper waste disposal, sanitation, and hygiene services for all communities through:

  • Sanitation Infrastructure

    Developing and improving sanitation facilities in schools, public spaces, and communities.

  • Solid Waste Management

    Implementing waste collection, segregation, and recycling initiatives to reduce environmental pollution.

  • Hygiene Promotion

    Conducting awareness campaigns and training on proper hygiene practices to prevent disease spread.

15+ schools supported

Climate Change and Environmental Protection

We promote sustainable practices and policies to protect the environment and address climate change through:

  • Climate Resilience

    Building community capacity to adapt to climate change impacts on water resources.

  • Ecosystem Conservation

    Protecting water catchment areas, wetlands, and other critical ecosystems in Kisumu County.

  • Green Technologies

    Promoting eco-friendly solutions for water and sanitation challenges.

3 ecosystem projects

Our Approach

We believe in a holistic, youth-led approach to addressing WASH challenges in Kisumu County.

Advocacy

Influencing policy and decision-making processes to prioritize WASH issues affecting youth and communities.

Capacity Building

Equipping youth with knowledge, skills, and resources to lead WASH initiatives in their communities.

Implementation

Executing practical projects that directly address WASH challenges in Kisumu County.
