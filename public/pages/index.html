About Us

KYWP is a youth-led and implementation body that seeks to bridge the gap between policy and action by combining advocacy efforts with prectical solutions

Our Mission

To advocate for and implement youth-led sustainable WASH initiatives

Our Vision

To be the leader in empowering youth to influence sustainable management and access of water, sanitation, and hygiene in Kisumu County.

Our Objectives

To Increase Awareness of WASH Issues Among Youth

To Foster Partnerships with Local Organizations and Influencers

To Develop Youth Capacity in Leadership and Advocacy

To Create Opportunities for Youth Participation in WASH Decision-Making Processes

Our Impact

See how our initiatives are transforming communities and improving lives across Kisumu.

School WASH education programme

We are advancing School WASH education by raising awareness among learners on hygiene, sanitation, and safe water practices, building the capacity of school communities through training and engagement, supporting the development of child-friendly WASH facilities, and advocating for policies that promote healthy and inclusive learning elvironments.

Read More

Journey of water campaign

We participated in the Journey of Water campaign dubbed (Catchment to tap) to raise awareness about the importance of River Kibos, educating communities on water conservation and pollution prevention, engaging stakeholders through a river walk experience, highlighting the connection between upstream and downstream users, and advocating for the protection and sustainable management of local water sources.

Read More

Reforestation of Kajulu forest

We promoted environmental restoration through the reforestation of Kajulu Forest by raising community awareness on the importance of forest ecosystems, mobilizing youth and local stakeholders for tree planting activities, planting over 5,000 indigenous tree species, and advocating for sustainable forest management and climate resilience.

Read More

Youth Innovation Summit

KYWP convened the National Youth Climate Change and Water Security Innovation Summit to empower young people to develop innovative solutions addressing climate resilience and water security. The summit brought together youth leaders, innovators, policymakers, and experts to share ideas, showcase climate-smart technologies, and foster collaboration towards sustainable water and climate action.

Read More

Waste management

KYWP led a waste management cleanup campaign around Lake Victoria and the Oboch area to address pollution and promote environmental stewardship. The initiative engaged local youth and community members in cleaning public spaces, raising awareness on proper waste disposal, advocating for reduced plastic use, and contributing to the protection of vital water bodies and ecosystems.

Read More

Policy

We have actively engaged in policy advocacy by submitting memoranda on key legislative matters including the Wetlands Regulation, Finance Bill, and Youth Bill. Through inclusive consultations and youth-led dialogues, the organization amplified young people's voices in decision-making processes, championed the protection of natural ecosystems, and advocated for equitable financing and youth-responsive policies at both local and national levels.
