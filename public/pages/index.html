About Us

KYWP is a youth-led and implementation body that seeks to bridge the gap between policy and action by combining advocacy efforts with prectical solutions

Our Mission

To advocate for and implement youth-led sustainable WASH initiatives

Our Vision

To be the leader in empowering youth to influence sustainable management and access of water, sanitation, and hygiene in Kisumu County.

Our Objectives

To Increase Awareness of WASH Issues Among Youth

To Foster Partnerships with Local Organizations and Influencers

To Develop Youth Capacity in Leadership and Advocacy

To Create Opportunities for Youth Participation in WASH Decision-Making Processes

Join Our Mission

Our Thematic Areas

Our work focuses on these three key thematic areas to create sustainable impact.

Integrated Water Resource Management(IWRM)

We are promoting IWRM by raising awareness through education and campaigns, building local capacity on water governance. collaborate with stakeholders, implement water-smart WASH projects, and advocate for inclusive, sustainable water policies.

Learn more

WASH

We implement comprehensive WASH programs, focusing on water supply, sanitation, and hygiene education to improve community health and well-being.

Learn more

Climate Action

We are promoting climate action by raising awareness on climate change, empowering communities with adaptation strategies, engaging youth in climate-smart initiatives, and advocating for policies that build resilience and environmental sustainability.

Learn more

Research and Innovation

We leverage innovation and research by developing sustainable, low-cost technologies, using data and evidence to inform solutions, engaging communities in co-creating ideas, and collaborating with institutions to scale impactful sanitation and hygiene initiatives.

Learn more

Waste Management

We are advancing waste management by educating communities on proper waste disposal, supporting clean-up drives and recycling initiatives, promoting circular economy practices, and advocating for sustainable urban and rural sanitation policies.

Learn more

Our Impact

Explore our impactful initiatives that are transforming communities and driving sustainable change

Youth Devolution Festival

KYWP organized the 2nd Youth Devolution Festival, engaging 500+ youth leaders in policy advocacy, skill development, and community engagement. The festival aimed to raise awareness on youth empowerment, promote sustainable development, and build a network of youth leaders for positive change.

Learn More

Kisumu Central NG-CDF project proposal

KYWP submitted a proposal for the Kisumu Central NG-CDF project, focusing on water and sanitation infrastructure development, training 100+ youth in construction and maintenance skills, and engaging local communities in project implementation. The project aimed to improve access to clean water and sanitation facilities, enhance community hygiene, and promote sustainable development.

Learn More

Kajulu Forest Restoration

Restoring 50 hectares of degraded forest land. 2,000+ trees planted, creating sustainable livelihoods for local youth.

Learn More

International Day of Peace

Engaging 500+ youth in peace-building activities focused on water resource management and conflict resolution.

Learn More

Fish Fiesta Collaboration

Educating 200+ fishermen about sustainable practices, reducing destructive fishing by 30% in targeted areas.

Learn More

KWAHO Collaboration

Implementing rainwater harvesting in 15 schools, training 100+ youth leaders in water management.

Learn More

Kibos WASH School Outreach

KYWP partnered with the Kibos Water and Sanitation Network (KEWASNET) to implement a WASH school outreach program. The initiative reached over 1,000 students, providing hygiene education, installing handwashing facilities, and training teachers on water and sanitation best practices. This program enhanced hygiene practices and reduced waterborne illnesses.

Learn More

Youth Innovation Summit

KYWP convened the National Youth Climate Change and Water Security Innovation Summit to empower young people to develop innovative solutions addressing climate resilience and water security. The summit brought together youth leaders, innovators, policymakers, and experts to share ideas, showcase climate-smart technologies, and foster collaboration towards sustainable water and climate action.

Learn More

LREB Water Conference 2024

KYWP convened the LREB Water Conference 2024 to address water challenges and promote sustainable water management. The conference featured expert speakers, interactive sessions, and networking opportunities, fostering collaboration and knowledge sharing to improve water security and resource management.
