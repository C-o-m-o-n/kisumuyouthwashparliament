About Us

Our Mission

The Kisumu Youth WASH Parliament is dedicated to empowering young people to take leadership roles in addressing water, sanitation, and hygiene challenges in their communities.

Advocating for clean water access in underserved areas

Promoting sustainable sanitation practices

Training youth in WASH leadership

Building partnerships for community development

Join Our Mission

Our Thematic Areas

Our work focuses on these three key thematic areas to create sustainable impact.

Integrated water resource management

Promoting effective policies and management systems for sustainable water resources and services.

Learn more

Waste management

Ensuring accessible and proper waste disposal, sanitation, and hygiene services for all communities.

Climate change and environmental protection.
