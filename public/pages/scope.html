Our Scope

Our Scope of Work

The Kisumu Youth WASH Parliament operates within a comprehensive framework to address water, sanitation, and hygiene challenges in Kisumu County.

Geographical Scope

Our primary focus is on Kisumu County, Kenya, with particular emphasis on:

  • Urban areas facing water scarcity and sanitation challenges
  • Informal settlements with limited access to WASH facilities
  • Rural communities with underdeveloped water infrastructure
  • Areas surrounding Lake Victoria affected by water pollution
  • Schools and public institutions requiring WASH improvements

Operational Scope

Our operations encompass a wide range of activities:

Advocacy

  • Policy development and reform
  • Youth representation in WASH governance
  • Budget allocation for WASH initiatives

Implementation

  • Water access projects
  • Sanitation facility construction
  • Hygiene promotion campaigns

Capacity Building

  • Youth leadership development
  • Technical skills training
  • Community mobilization

Research & Innovation

  • WASH needs assessments
  • Sustainable solutions development
  • Impact evaluation

Target Beneficiaries

Our initiatives are designed to benefit:

Youth

Ages 18-35, empowering them as WASH leaders and advocates

Schools

Primary and secondary institutions requiring improved WASH facilities

Communities

Underserved populations with limited access to clean water and sanitation
