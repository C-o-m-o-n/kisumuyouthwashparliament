Our Mission

The Kisumu Youth WASH Parliament is dedicated to empowering young people to take leadership roles in addressing water, sanitation, and hygiene challenges in their communities.

Advocating for clean water access in underserved areas

Promoting sustainable sanitation practices

Training youth in WASH leadership

Building partnerships for community development

Join Our Mission

Waste Management
