POLICY ADVOCACY

Water Policy Advocacy

Reducing Non-Revenue Water (NRW) loss is crucial for ensuring sustainable water management in Kisumu. During our productive discussion with Kisumu Water and Sanitation Company Limited (KIWASCO), we explored how Parliament can play an active role in addressing the 38% NRW loss through legislative frameworks, policy oversight, and infrastructure improvements.

EDUCATION & ADVOCACY

Sensitization and Advocacy

We conducted an interactive session with students at Mbeme Primary School, Kisumu East, focusing on water, sanitation, and waste management best practices. Teaching these concepts at an early age helps instill lifelong habits that contribute to healthier communities and environmental sustainability.

Partnerships and Collaboration

We were honored to participate in the WASH Benchmarking Forum in Lusaka, Zambia, represented by Adwet Mc'Omondi. This forum provided a platform for sharing expertise, fostering innovation, and building partnerships for sustainable water, sanitation, and hygiene solutions.
